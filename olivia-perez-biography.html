Who is Olivia Pérez?

Olivia Pérez is an American actress who started to act at an early age. Olivia Pérez is known for acting in projects like In the Heights (2021), Swallow (2019), Christmas in Wonderland (2022), etc.

Recently, she co-hosted a kid’s show titled The Kids Tonight Show. Her movies Christmas in Wonderland is in the post-production stage. In 2021, she was seen in an episode of The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon.
