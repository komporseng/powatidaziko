Gillian Jacobs career earnings and networth

The famous Gillian MacLaren Jacobs is better known as an American actress and director. She directed the 2015 documentary short The Queen of Code about computer scientist and United States Navy rear admiral Grace Hopper. In 2017, Jacobs co-starred in Janicza Bravo’s first full-length feature, Lemon, which debuted at Sundance Film Festival. In 2018, she directed Curated, a narrative film short, as a part of a series produced by TNT and Refinery29.

What is Gillian Jacobs’s net worth?

Gillian Jacobs has an estimated net worth of about $5 million. Her source of wealth is being an American actress, director, and producer.

Gillian Jacobs is best known for portraying Britta Perry on the NBC sitcom Community (2009–2015), Mimi-Rose Howard on the fourth season of the HBO comedy-drama series Girls (2015), Mickey Dobbs on the Netflix romantic comedy series Love (2016–2018), and the voice of Atom Eve on the Amazon animated series Invincible (2021–present). She has also appeared in films such as Gardens of the Night (2008), Life Partners (2014), Don’t Think Twice (2016), Ibiza (2018), I Used to Go Here (2020), and The Fear Street Trilogy (2021).
