NIKKE x Chainsaw Man Collaboration Announced

The long-rumored NIKKE x Chainsaw Man collaboration event has finally been officially confirmed by Crunchyroll and Shift Up. Chainsaw Man characters will make guest appearances in NIKKE: Goddess of Victory media and will even get a new playable SSR based on a character in the anime. Here's everything you need to know about this new collaboration.

Announced accompanied by a new promotional video, the NIKKE x Chainsaw Man collaboration event will bring three members of the Public Safety Department Special Division 4 from the anime to the game as new characters. The crossover event will be fully playable from February 22, 2023, to March 14, 2023.

The confirmed new characters will be Makima, Power, and Himeno. Both Makima and Power will be SSR characters. Here are how you can recruit each of the different new characters in the game:

How to Recruit Makima (SSR): Limited-Time Recruitment Banner (Available until March 15, 2023)
How to Recruit Power (SSR): Limited-Time Recruitment Banner (Available until March 15, 2023)
How to Recruit Himeno (SR): Acquired through the Chainsaw Man Event: obtained as part of the rewards of the 14-day log-in campaign.

As part of the event, NIKKE introduces a new manufacturer, particularly to accommodate these three new characters into the game: Abnormal. These come with new Recycling Rooms, bond gifts, and Manufacturer Equipment specific to these characters. This will not unlock a new Tribe Tower for the new NIKKE.

Coinciding with this event is an online sweepstake that will reward players with a Chainsaw Man Keychain Set. The sweepstake promotion will run throughout the collaboration event dates.

Also coinciding with the NIKKE x Chainsaw Man collaboration announcement is the launch of the PC version of NIKKE. For more details about this, you can check out our article on the NIKKE PC version launch. For everything else about gaming and esports, stick with ClutchPoints Gaming.
