Top 22 Jimmy Garoppolo Quotes

Jimmy Garoppolo is a renowned American football quarterback who plays for the San Francisco 49ers of the National Football League (NFL). During college football, he had won the Walter Payton Award as the best offensive player in the Football Championship Subdivision (FCS).

Moreover, he was associated with the New England Patriots and was the backup to Tom Brady in two Super Bowls. He achieved a lot in being linked with the teams. Now, visit his top 22 quotes that will inspire you to win.
