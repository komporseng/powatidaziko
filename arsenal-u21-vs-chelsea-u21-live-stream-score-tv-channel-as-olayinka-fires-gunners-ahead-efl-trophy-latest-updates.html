Arsenal U21 vs Chelsea U21 LIVE: Stream, score, TV channel as Olayinka fires Gunners ahead EFL T

STARS OF THE FUTURE

What a chance for Chelsea and Arsenal’s young guns to put on a show tonight.

The two under-21 sides clash in the EFL Trophy this evening, with Meadow Park playing host to the knockout clash.

A place in the quarter-finals is up for grabs, where Wigan await following the Latics emphatic 6-0 win over Oldham.

This competition sees a mixture of League Two, League One and Premier League B teams battle it out for silverware.

And after making it safely the group stages, both Arsenal and Chelsea will be eyeing a run to Wembley.

The Gunners were arguably somewhat fortunate to qualify, taking four points from a possible nine.

Chelsea meanwhile were slightly more impressive, beating Bristol Rovers and then seeing off Exeter on penalties.

We’re live tonight from North London as the stars of tomorrow look to shine today.

Team news isn’t far away, kick-off is at 7pm GMT.
