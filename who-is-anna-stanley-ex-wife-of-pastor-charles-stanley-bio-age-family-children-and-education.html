Anna Stanley education, career, and family

Anna Stanley was born on the 9th of October in 1931 and she hails from Smithfield, North Carolina. Anna finished her college studies at Richmond Professional Institute where she completed her graduation with a degree in Art. After completing graduation, Anna Stanley began working as a Bible teacher at George Mueller Christian School.

She got married to Charles Stanley in early 1955. They both have a son named Andy Stanley who is a pastor of the North Point Community Church which is situated in Alpharetta, Georgia, and a daughter, Becky Stanley. Apart from being happily married for decades, the duo started facing conjugal disputes after shifting to Atlanta. Charles became busy with ministry work and started neglecting his family.

After 40 years of marriage, Anna Stanley filed for the divorce on 22nd of June in 1993. Afterward, the duo started leading separate lives for almost a year. The duo then mutually agreed to amend the lawsuit to legal separation.

Moreover, Anna Stanley again filed for her divorce on 20th March 1995. That time Anna wrote a letter to her estranged husband’s church explaining the reasons for separation. Divorce papers were filed for the last time on the 16th of February in 2000 while the judge granted their divorce on the 11th of May in 2000.

The divorce has ended up stirring quite a bit of controversy in the Southern Baptist Convention as Anna Stanley had earlier claimed,’ he would be now resigning as a pastor if he became a divorcee.’
