Who is Young Mazino?

Young Mazino is a Korean-American Actor. Young is best known for the role of Sanggun in The Destined King (Short) in 2016, Armored Guard in Blindspot (TV Series) in 2016, and Peter Kim in Fish Bones (Drama Romance) in 2018.

Mazino appeared as Paul Cho in the critically acclaimed black comedy, Beef (2023).
