Julianne Houghs Pink Wig At UNICEF Event See Her Bold Look For Hallowee Hollywood Life

Julianne Hough, 28, was practically unrecognizable as she played dress up for UNICEF’s charity event in Los Angeles on Oct. 27. The dancing pro can be seen sporting long blonde locks every week on ABC’s DWTS, which is why it threw us off when she was pictured in a short bob style that was also cotton candy colored.

Opting for a sleek bob wig whose ends curled under around her chin, Jules’ chosen style also included bangs that covered her brows. Making her bright pink look even bolder, Julianne decided to throw on a deep red lipstick and ’70s style round sunglasses.

We’re not sure what exactly she was dressing up as, but Jules definitely looked glam wearing a black lace bodysuit, high-waisted pants and a fur coat. Plus, it was fun to see her in a completely different look than we’re used to.

Even though Jules loves to play with different styles each week on DWTS, she’s never tried such a bright hair color before. This season alone, Jules and her Nine Zero One Salon hairstylists have tried everything from a chic slicked back top knot that included a cool twisted braid to an insane messy braided updo that included crystal hair jewelry woven throughout.
