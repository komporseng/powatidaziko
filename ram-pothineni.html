Ram Pothineni is an energetic star who exclusively works in the Telugu cinema and has performed in many Telugu movies for which he gained huge acclaim. While he is mainly known for the role of Devdas in the most popular Telugu movie “Devadasu” which was released in the year 2006. He was born on 15th May 1988, hailing from Hyderabad, India. He had a passion for acting since childhood and that’s a reason he decided to work in the same field. He has also worked in the Tamil cinema and has appeared in many movies.

Career

In 2006, Ram took the start of his career and appeared in a Telugu debut movie “Davadasu” in which he appeared as Dev. For this role, he gained much recognition for the brilliant performance and since then, he frequently appeared in many movies. Later, he marked his identity in the Tamil cinema where he appeared in Tamil movies and became the king of millions of hearts across the nation. Undoubtedly, the young, charming actor has the ability to win the hearts with his excellent acting prowess. In 2002, he made his Tamil debut movie “Adayaalam” which was considered as the best release of the year. His many fans noted him for his incredible performances and praise him for the amazing contribution to the Tamil and Telugu cinema. However, he has acted in many movies and enthralled a large number of audiences with his remarkable performances. The charismatic star has achieved so much in his career life and still, he is struggling to accomplish his career goals. He has won the award for giving the best performance in the Tamil movie “Adayaalam” and he calls it one of the major life achievements.

Achievements

Among various awards and honors, Ram Pothineni was honored with the Best Actor Award for the Tamil debut movie “Adayaalam” in European Movies Festival.
