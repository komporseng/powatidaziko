Conor McGregors multi-million-pound car collection: From Rolls Royce he bought after Jose Aldo KO

Conor McGregor’s most famous supercar is his £2.9 million Lamborghini yacht, but he’s got an impressive fleet waiting when he docks.

The former dual-weight UFC champion, who recently revealed he has made around £520m during his 10-year UFC run, unsurprisingly owns an insane collection of luxury cars.

McGregor’s massive fleet is worth millions and will certainly continue to grow as he edges closer to his eagerly anticipated return from injury.

‘Notorious’ famously got a new set of wheels before beating lightweight champion Eddie Alvarez at UFC 205 in 2016, and will surely add to his collection ahead of his clash with Michael Chandler later this year.

Rolls Royce

Two of McGregor’s favourite cars are Rolls Royce and he took ownership of them during the peak of his fight career.

In 2015, when he knocked out Jose Aldo in 13 seconds to become the undisputed featherweight champion, the MMA legend celebrated by purchasing a ‘Phantom Coupe’ worth around £305,000.

A year later, the luxury car brand got in touch with McGregor and gifted him a £238,000 ‘Ghost’ with his picture and nickname emblazoned across its exterior.

The 34-year-old is believed to own at least three more cars from the Rolls Royce collection that are worth in excess of £875,000.

Lamborghini

Lamborghini is the car brand used most by the McGregor clan.

The former featherweight and lightweight champion is the proud owner of two ‘Aventadors' – one is convertible - worth £276,000 and he has also been seen driving a £185,000 ‘Huracan Avio’ in the past.

On Christmas Eve 2019, he shared a picture of the ‘Urus’ SUV he was giving his long-time partner Dee Devlin – a £154,000 gift she was surely delighted with.

Even McGregor’s son has got a Lamborghini and it became a talking point when eagled-eyed viewers of his recently released Netflix documentary noticed the toy car had a unique license plate referencing his best win.

Bentley

‘Mystic Mac’ almost lost one of his favourite cars in 2022

McGregor, who is often pictured in his £170,000 Bentley ‘Continental GT’, was arrested while driving through west Dublin in it last March.

Irish police accused him of dangerous driving and temporarily confiscated the vehicle but handed it back even though the case against the UFC icon is still ongoing.

Later in the year, McGregor drove the same car to his Black Forged Inn pub to watch the World Cup final between Argentina and France.

Wait, there’s more…

While McGregor certainly has his favourites, he’s simply a fan of supercars and is often seen driving around in whatever takes his fancy.

The Irishman owns a BMW i8 worth £114,200, a £200,000 McLaren 650S, and a Mercedes-AMG S550 Coupe which goes for around £110,000.

Of course, no sports star’s car collection would be complete without a Range Rover. McGregor has owned several over the years and kitted his vehicles out with all the best specifications bringing their value to £90,000.

In 2021, he bought his 20-year-old niece a Range Rover, showing that it's pretty much impossible to know exactly how many cars he actually owns as many of them are in the hands of family and friends.
