published on May 01, 2024
From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia. Cotton Guard (Japanese: コットンガード Cotton Guard) is a non-damaging Grass-type move introduced in Generation V. Effect Cotton Guard raises the user's Defense stat by three stages. Cotton Guard can also be used as part of a Contest Spectacular combination, causing Baton Pass and Stored Power to give the user an extra three appeal points if they are used in the next turn.
