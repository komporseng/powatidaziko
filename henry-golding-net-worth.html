Henry Golding Bio & Family

The actor was born as Henry Ewan Golding on 5 February 1987 in Sarawak, Malaysia. His parents Clive Golding and Margaret Likan Golding raised him. Henry’s family for five long years lived in Terengganu, on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia. They then moved to Surrey, England, when Henry was eight years of age.

Henry went to The Warwick School, Redhill. The artist stands at the height of 6 feet 1.25 inches (1.86 m) tall and weighs around 81 kg.
