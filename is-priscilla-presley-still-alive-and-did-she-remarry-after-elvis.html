Is Priscilla Presley still alive?

According to Distractify, Elvis first met her at a party in West Germany in 1959, when he was 24 and she was 14.

Her parents didn’t like that she was dating the famous musician at the time, but they spent a lot of time with him before he went back to the United States in 1960.

Even though Elvis was very busy with his career, they still talked often before they met again in Los Angeles in 1962.
