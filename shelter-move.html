Shelter (Japanese: たてこもる Barricade) is a non-damaging Steel-type move introduced in Pokémon Legends: Arceus. It is the signature move of Goomy and its Hisuian evolutions.

Effect

Generation VIII

Shelter raises the user's defensive stats in addition to making opponents' moves less likely to hit the user.

Generation IX

Shelter raises the user's Defense by two stages.
